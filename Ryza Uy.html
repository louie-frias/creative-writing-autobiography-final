Ryza Uy

I am Ryza Uy, 17 years old. When I grow up I love being with my Grandmother she always spoil me then when my lola died I'm always feel empty inside me. Sometimes I ask myself why should people suffer for things they can't handle, I really love being with my grandma in short she's my comfort zone and my beautiful lola.

My mother always ask me why my personality is change, I change because of my grandma all I need is my grandma. My father always yell at me to do cleaning in the house even though I need them by my side, they can't understand my feelings everyday that's why all I want is my grandma. My story is being with my grandma because she's always on my mind when I'm young and still now need her to be by my side I want her to be proud of me even my mother can't understand my feelings sometimes. I need comfort from my parents but they always busy and they giving me a sign that my mother and father is not interested for saying those things about my problem in life.

It's really hard to see other kids that always with there parents yes, sometimes I felt jealous because all I want is my parents to be with me. But ended up to chose my lola to be on my side everyday, I love seeing my lola happy and laughing even though I'm suffering in this world.

But I'm thankful that I have someone in my life and friends that always by my side everyday, I love being around with them, they make me feel special everytime. I'm glad I have parents even though they have no idea how I suffer in my life, I love them because they are my family, and since my lover came to my life. I feel like, my life is getting bright when I meet him. Yes, sometimes our relationship is not okay. But, it's okay in the end we still chose to fix our relationship and ended up to healthy relationship.

That's why I need to be strong for them even my family hurt me I still need them, even my grandma is gone I need to live for her, it's hard but that's life I live for her, and I love being with people that makes me special.
